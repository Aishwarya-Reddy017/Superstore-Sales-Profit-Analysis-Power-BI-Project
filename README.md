📊 **Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard**

### Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of Superstore sales data through interactive dashboards. The project focuses on business performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product analysis to support data-driven decision-making.

### 📌 **Project Objectives**

* Analyze sales and profit performance.
* Monitor KPIs and business trends.
* Identify top-performing products and customers.
* Evaluate regional performance.
* Study customer purchasing behavior.
* Analyze discount impact on profitability.

### 📈 **Dashboard Pages**

#### **1. Executive Overview**

Provides a summary of:

* **Total Sales**
* **Total Profit**
* **Total Orders**
* **Profit Margin**
* **Average Order Value**

#### **2. Sales Analytics**

* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Segment Analysis

#### **3. Profit Analytics**

* Profit Margin Analysis
* Profit by Region
* Profit by Product
* Category Profit Analysis
* Yearly Profit Trends

#### **4. Customer Analytics**

* Customer Purchase Patterns
* Top Customers
* Segment Analysis
* Geographic Analysis

#### **5. Product Analytics**

* Product Performance
* Category Contribution
* Sub-Category Analysis
* Discount Impact on Profit

### 📊 **Key Insights**

* **Total Sales: 2.30M**
* **Total Profit: 286.40K**
* **Total Orders: 5K**
* **Profit Margin: 12%**
* **West Region generated the highest sales and profit.**
* **Consumer Segment contributed the highest sales.**
* **Sales steadily increased from 2014 to 2017.**
* **Discounts significantly influenced profitability.**
